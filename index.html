grandma Bruyn's Zucchini Pie

today we will be learning how to make a delicious zucchini pie. Great for a unique family dinner experience

  • start by heating your gas or electric oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit

    • the ingredients you will need

    • 4 cups zucchini
      • shredded
      • or chopped
    • 1 cup of bisquick
    • 4 eggs (beaten)
    • 1/2 cup oil
    • 1/2 cup parm. cheese
    • 2tps persley

    steps to prepare your pie

    1. mix well
    2. place in large pie dish
    3. cook for 45 mins
    4. let sit for 10 mins
